### Nova Product Ops QA Perfomance Dashboard — Case Study

#### THE PROBLEM
Product and QA teams often lack a single view connecting release timing, bug backlog, and customer happiness...so problems get caught late.

#### WHAT I DID
- Modeled and queried a product operations dataset in SQL.
- Built KPI logic in Power BI (DAX) to calculate on-time delivery %, average bug resolution time, and satisfaction trend.
- Designed an interactive dashboard so leadership can filter by product module and see delivery health at a glance

#### WHAT IT FOUND
- 93.8% on-time delivery
- ~14 bugs/release average
- Satisfaction rising from 3.1 to 4.3 over the year 2025
- IoT Sensor Hub had the lowest on-time rate, suggesting that module needs more QA resourcing before release.

#### WHY IT MATTERS
- This mirrors the kind of dashboard I build in my Product Operations Analyst role at NovaTrack, where I tracked release delivery and bug resolution across telematics/IoT products and report these metrics weekly to developers and management.

#### TOOLS
- SQL
- Python
- Power BI
- DAX
